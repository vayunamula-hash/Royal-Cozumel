Problem

Guests view Cozumel as a standard port stop.

Low awareness + low pre-booking rates → missed revenue opportunities.

What we’re positioning

A seamless cruise-to-shore premium escape—so the beach club feels like a core part of the vacation, not an optional add-on.

Social + Influencer: 39.3% Consumer/Endorsements: 33.5% FIFA World Cup: 27.2%

Campaign Objectives

Benchmarks are designed to increase awareness, drive pre-booking, and scale digital engagement.

1) Increase awareness & bookings

Benchmark: Boost awareness of Royal Caribbean itineraries and Royal Beach Club Cozumel by 30%, and bookings by 25% in 45 days.

  • Highlight the beach club as the premier shore experience
  • Position Cozumel as a must-visit destination within itineraries
  • Emphasize the seamless connection between onboard luxury + shore-side experiences
  • Make the beach club feel like a core part of the vacation

2) Expand digital reach & engagement

Benchmark: Reach and engage at least 100,000 potential customers through digital, social, and email channels.

  • Influencer partnerships + multi-platform content to expand reach
  • Targeted content to convert cruise planners into pre-bookers
  • UTM tracking + conversion pixels for attribution

3) Leverage strategic partnerships & promotions

Benchmark: Collaborate with at least five travel influencers and align promotions with the FIFA World Cup 2026 to maximize global reach.

  • High-quality visuals + influencer storytelling to build trust and desire
  • Celebrity integrations to add credibility + cultural relevance
  • Interactive polls + limited-time incentives to reduce booking hesitation

Target Markets

Primary = Royal Caribbean cruise guests. Secondary = destination-focused travelers drawn to elevated beach experiences.

Primary Market: Royal Caribbean cruise guests

  • Who: Guests sailing on Royal Caribbean itineraries to Cozumel
  • Mindset: Value relaxation, exclusivity, and entertainment
  • Preference: Seamless, all-inclusive shore experiences
  • Segments: Families, couples, luxury-focused travelers
  • Offer angle: Bundled cruise + beach club packages and premium upgrades

Secondary Market: Destination-focused travelers

  • Who: Domestic & international travelers interested in Cozumel
  • Mindset: Drawn to curated, high-quality experiences
  • Preference: Comfort, convenience, and exclusivity
  • Segments: Couples and experience-driven tourists
  • Offer angle: Influencer content + celebrity events + limited-time discounts

Campaign Activities

Five major pillars that create global awareness, cultural relevance, and high-intent conversion.

FIFA World Cup Global Promotion

  • Broadcast ads during matches spotlight luxury, beaches, and ship-to-shore ease
  • Airport and major city billboards extend awareness during travel planning moments
  • Emotion-led storytelling aligned with World Cup unity/celebration
Budget: $1,400,000 (Broadcast $1,000,000 + Billboards/Digital $400,000)

Celebrity Endorsements & “The Weeknd” Tour Partnership

  • Celebrity endorsements to build trust + aspiration (Johnny Depp, Zendaya, Selena Gomez)
  • The Weeknd tour partnership for high-frequency exposure & global pop-culture alignment
  • Launch messaging across planning, onboard, and post-sailing touchpoints
Budget: Weeknd Partnership $500,000; Celebrity endorsements $300,000

Social Media & Short-Form Content Marketing

  • TikTok + Instagram Reels: 15–30s clips of cabanas, swim-up bars, dining
  • YouTube Shorts + UGC: hashtag campaigns (#MyRoyalBeachDay, #CozumelLuxury)
  • Contests: “Dream Beach Day,” “Tag a Travel Buddy,” photo/video submissions
  • Cross-platform repurposing + A/B testing + weekly performance reporting
Budget: $10,000 content + $10,000 giveaways (within Social/Influencer bucket)

Influencer Cruise Experience

  • Creators experience ship + beach club end-to-end and produce authentic content
  • Distributed across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, blogs, and more for multi-platform reach
  • Real-time Q&As, tours, polls, and genuine reviews build trust like peer recommendations
Budget: $1,500,000

Interactive Engagement & Promotional Incentives

Interactive polls (in-app + onboard screens)

  • Guests vote on beach-day activities (sports, entertainment, dining themes)
  • Progressive voting improves future sailings based on feedback
  • Gamified participation + prize drawings to maximize engagement
Budget: $5,000

Discount packages (conversion focus)

  • Limited-time discounts reduce hesitation to pre-book during planning
  • Bundles pair beach club access with popular cruise add-ons
  • Creates urgency, lifts volume, and increases attach rate for upgrades
Budget: $15,000
